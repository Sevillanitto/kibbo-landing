Most consumer products are designed to be confusing. A supplement label lists thirty ingredients in microscopic type; a contract buries the clause that matters on page nine; a job offer frames a below-market salary as the opportunity of a lifetime. The information you need is technically there — it’s just deliberately hard to parse.

Kibbo’s analyzers do the parsing for you. Paste the text and an AI model trained to read these documents critically returns a plain-English breakdown: what each part means, what’s normal, and what should give you pause. No account, no upsell, nothing stored.
