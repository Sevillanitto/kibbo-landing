Consumer Rights by Country

Your rights depend on where you are

The same problem has different rules in the UK and the US. Pick your country and go straight to the resources, guides, tools and templates that actually apply to you.

A refund window, a complaint deadline, the authority with the power to force a company's hand — all of it changes at the border. This page splits Kibbo's consumer-protection content by country so you're never reading US-only advice for a UK problem, or vice versa. Right now that's the UK and the US, the two jurisdictions we have real, verified content for. Each column links to the official authorities, our Consumer Rights Wizard pre-set to that country, the country-specific guides, and any templates that are exclusive to it.
