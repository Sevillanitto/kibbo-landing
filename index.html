FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Why are Pro features free right now? +
Kibbo is in Beta. We need real feedback. You help us improve; we give you full access. When we launch the stable version, early users will receive a special discount or lifetime access.
Does Kibbo collect my browsing data? +
No. All analysis is done locally in your browser. We can't see your passwords or chats. We only analyze visible prices on the page to protect you.
Does it slow down browsing? +
Not noticeably. The engine uses a 250ms throttle and filters out irrelevant elements. The impact on load time is less than 1%.
Does it work on all websites? +
Yes. The text, price, and T&C detectors are generic and work on any website in Spanish and English, with advanced recognition for over 100 stores.
Are the comparator links affiliate links? +
Some are. Kibbo may receive a small commission. We always show all relevant options, with or without affiliate links. Transparency is part of the product.
Which browsers does it support? +
Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Brave, and Opera. Installation is the same on all of them.
